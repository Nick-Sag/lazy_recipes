Omelette like no other!

Ingredients

  • Grass fed eggs without GMO!
  • Sea salt from bermuda triangle
  • Saint Maria's extra virgin oil
  • Some salami, veggies, bacon or any food which you like

Preparation

  1. Mix the eggs together
  2. Heat the pan and add oil
  3. Pour the egg mixture to the pan and cover it, wait 5 minutes
  4. Chop the meat, veggies or any food which you like
  5. Add the food on one half of the omelette and then cover with the other half, should look like moon or taco. Enjoy!
