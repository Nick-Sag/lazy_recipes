The easiest to make sandwich, which tastes good!

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Butter or cream cheese
  • Salami, cheese, any type of sliced veggies or even canned sardines!

Preparation

  1. Cut the bead in square shape or buy cut one
  2. Spread butter on bread or cream cheese
  3. Add the finishing slice of food which happens to be at your house and enjoy!
